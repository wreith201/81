Welcome to No. War.

This is a link to an independent game demo.

How to Play

1. Tap or click random number grids to attack your opponent.

2. Attack point = sum value of the Cross-shaped region - opponent's corresponding column.

3. Green sidebar means that it is your turn to play. Red sidebar means it is your opponent's turn.

4. Tap Left-Bottom bar to activate AI control for next turn.
