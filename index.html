How to Play

1. Tap or click random number grids to attack your opponent.

2. Attack point = sum value of the Cross-shaped region - sum value of opponent's corresponding column.

3. Green sidebar indicatess that it is your turn to play. Red sidebar indicates it is your opponent's turn.

4. Tap Left-Bottom bar to activate AI control for next turn.
